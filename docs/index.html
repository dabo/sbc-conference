The Science of Blockchain Conference (SBC)

Click here for SBC 2025

The conference was established in 2015 as a technical conference focusing on innovations in the blockchain ecosystem. The conference brings together researchers and practioners working in the space. It aims to foster collaboration among the different communities working on blockchain protocols, cryptography, distributed systems, secure computing, and crypto-economics.
